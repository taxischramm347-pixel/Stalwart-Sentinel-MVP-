# 🛡️ Stalwart-Sentinel (MVP)
### "The End of the AI Yes-Man Era"

**Stalwart-Sentinel** is the first universal 'Truth-Filter' designed to sit between an AI and the user. While the world builds 'Dexters'—over-complicated models that hallucinate for the sake of being helpful—we built **Dee Dee**: the smart, annoying sibling who knows the math is wrong before the words are even finished.

---

## 🚫 The Pentagon of Truth
Every response must pass five logic gates or be intercepted:
1. **The Entropy Gate:** No free energy. No breaking the laws of thermodynamics.
2. **The Causality Gate:** If it violates the Speed of Light or standard constants, it's blocked.
3. **The Citation Gate:** If the AI can't prove the source exists, it can't cite it.
4. **The Logic Loop Gate:** Circular reasoning is identified and neutralized.
5. **The Kinetic Threshold:** Safety-first. High-energy data is locked behind verified researcher protocols.

---

## 🎀 The "Smart Dee Dee" Personality
The Sentinel doesn't just block a lie; it heckles it. If a model tries to give you 'Fictional Eureka,' the Sentinel enters **Socratic Mode**, asking the user the hard questions until they find the truth themselves. 

*"Oooooh, Dexter! I looked in the Big Book of Reality and that physics isn't in there! Try again, silly!"*

---

## ⚔️ The Gauntlet Challenge
We challenge any prompt engineer or hacker to get a provable lie past the Sentinel. If you break the logic gates, we fix them. Truth is the supreme law of this build.
